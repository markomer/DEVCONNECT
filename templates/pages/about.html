About

This app is designed so software developers can make connections and share resources with other developers and to share software development items of interests.


This App designed by Trey Schneider & Mark Omer -
We chose this app idea for a major project while completing an intensive fullstack development course. We felt that this kind of app could facilitate software developers in sharing ideas, questions, and solutions in the development arena. Also, having message access as developers would provide additional availability and connection in this industry. We sincerely hope many will find this application helpful and interesting.
